<p><strong>The Extruded Baked Snacks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Extrusion</li><li>Cold Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extruded baked snacks are produced through two main methods: hot extrusion and cold extrusion. Hot extrusion involves cooking and forcing ingredients through a die at high temperatures, resulting in a light, airy texture often seen in products like puffs and chips. In contrast, cold extrusion combines ingredients without heat, allowing for a denser, chewier product, commonly found in crackers and forming specific shapes. Both methods cater to diverse consumer preferences while enhancing flavor and texture in snacks.</p></p>
