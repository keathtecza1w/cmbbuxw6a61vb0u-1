<p><strong>The Chickpea Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Desi</li><li>Kabuli</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chickpea flour is categorized mainly into two market types: Desi and Kabuli. Desi chickpeas are smaller, darker, and more flavorful, commonly used in traditional Indian and Middle Eastern cuisines, resulting in a flour with a robust taste. Kabuli chickpeas are larger, lighter, and paler, often preferred in Western markets for their milder flavor and smoother texture, suitable for various culinary applications. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and regional dishes, influencing pricing and availability in the market.</p></p>
