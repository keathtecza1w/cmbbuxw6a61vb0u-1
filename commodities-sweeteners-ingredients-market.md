<p><p>The commodities sweeteners ingredients market encompasses various artificial and natural sweeteners such as sucralose, aspartame, stevia, saccharin, and neotame. Stevia, a natural sweetener derived from plants, has gained popularity for its health benefits. Saccharin, one of the oldest sweeteners, is calorie-free. Neotame, a powerful artificial sweetener, is often used in processed foods for its intense sweetness.</p></p>
